About

Where we're going, we don't need fins.

nofins is freediving off Phuket — a way back to yourself. Your own buoy, your own pace, someone watching over you. Minimal gear, all the time in the world.

Freediving teaches the nervous system to settle. Slow the breath, drop down the line, and the ease you find there starts to follow you up — into your day, into whatever the day holds. You come up more alive. That's the part we care about, and the part we teach.

We dive out of Nama Freediving, a Molchanovs beachfront center on Panwa beach, and we teach the whole Molchanovs path — kids and adults, first-timer to freediver.

Where · Phuket, Thailand

Who · any level, first-timer to freediver

Gear · nothing is needed

Run by Nick — a developer who dives the line with you. Molchanovs Wave 2 instructor, static best 6:15.
