Thirteen years in the water and a photographer’s eye. Tony taught freediving on Koh Tao for seven years before moving to Phuket in 2022, and he brings a calm presence to every line.

His camera background shows up in the coaching — he reads your body position, your movement, your efficiency the way he’d frame a shot, and helps you refine it. Divers of all levels progress with him, safely and at their own pace.
