Senior Freediving

A way back to yourself — your own buoy, your own pace.

Just breath, body, and attention. We teach the calm side of the breath-hold — the steadiness that follows you back into your day, into whatever the day holds. Start where you are; the water meets you there, and you come up more alive. Molchanovs Wave 1 through 3, off Phuket.

Wave 1 — Beginner

Your first breath-holds and dives, safe and unhurried.

Wave 2 — Advanced

Deeper lines, cleaner technique, real ease at depth.

Wave 3 — Master

The deep end — for divers ready to go all the way.

The urge to breathe is a reflex — and once you feel that for yourself, the whole thing opens up. You learn to stay with it, relaxed, and the line becomes somewhere you want to be.

That ease is what you take back up — into your day, into whatever the day holds. You come up more alive, and it follows you.

We run two rhythms off Phuket: deep water on Saturdays — off the boat, down the line — and pool sessions on Sundays for technique and breath-work. One person per buoy, always your own. Come to either, or both.
