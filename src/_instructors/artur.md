A competing finswimmer turned freediving instructor — Artur has won national underwater-sport titles in both bi-fins and monofin, and he brings that athlete's precision to the pool.

Personal bests tell the story: 248 m bi-fins, 227 m monofin, 161 m breaststroke, and a 7:24 static. He teaches Wave 1 and Wave 2 courses in the pool, and he's a certified freediving rescue and first-aid specialist — the calm hand you want on safety.
