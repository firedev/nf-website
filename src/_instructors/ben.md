Twelve years travelling the world, and freediving is the thing that made him stop and stay. Benoit found it on a trip to Thailand in 2019, and it reshaped everything after.

He teaches breath-hold and yoga together — the two practices lean on each other, and he coaches from that overlap. Calm body, calm breath, one feeding the other.

On his days off you'll find him deep in his favourite discipline: static.
