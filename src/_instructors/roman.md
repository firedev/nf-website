Swimming and diving since childhood — Roman came up through competitive swimming and swam for his city team before freediving became the centre of it.

He's a competing freediver, still refining his own dives while he coaches. Certified to teach both adults (Wave 3) and kids (L1 & L2) in the Molchanovs system, and an experienced safety diver and coach.

He organises the amateur *I love freediving* competitions and works as sports manager at the Natalia Molchanova Freediving School.
