Nick's journey into freediving began as a path to overcome his own fears and anxiety. This personal transformation led him to develop a unique teaching methodology that combines traditional freediving techniques with mindfulness practices. His approach emphasizes mental preparation and relaxation over depth achievements, making freediving accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level.

Through Psychedelic Freediving, Nick has helped numerous students discover the meditative and transformative aspects of underwater exploration. His sessions focus on creating a safe, supportive environment where participants can explore their mental and physical boundaries at their own pace.
