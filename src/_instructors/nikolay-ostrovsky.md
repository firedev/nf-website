Freediving changed my life.

Twenty years a software developer, and I still build — [firedev.com](https://firedev.com) is mine. Freediving is what gives me the room back, dive after dive.

No fins, just breath, body, and attention. I teach the calm side of the breath-hold off Phuket — the part that follows you back to the desk, to the hard conversation, to the 3am wake-up.

I write about the thinking behind it at [think→forward](https://thinkforward.academy). Joy is the absence of fear — that's the whole thing, and the water is where I found it.
