Junior Freediving

Kids meet the water — breath, play, and calm. Ages 4 to 17.

Freediving gives a child room to grow. Steady breath, a strong body, a calm mind — learned in play, in the water, at their own pace. We teach the Molchanovs kids method, the same lineage the whole school comes from.

Body

Posture, lung capacity, and a real ease in the water.

Focus

Attention and concentration, built dive by dive.

Safety

How to swim and dive safely, from the first session.

Love of water

Care for the ocean and a lasting bond with it.

Comfort in the water is where a child begins. Before any skill, they get to simply be in it: unhurried, at their own pace, with someone they trust right there beside them. That ease is the whole point.

From there, everything else grows on its own. Steady breath, a strong body, real attention — all of it learned in play. A child who feels at home in the water carries that calm back out of it, into the rest of their day.

We teach the Molchanovs kids method, the same lineage the whole school comes from, out of Nama Freediving on Panwa beach. One kid at a time, at their own pace.
